Classes

The Phriendly Food and Drink Spot is pleased to offer a variety of classes to cooks and bartenders of all levels. We update our class themes to reflect seasonal ingredients as well as a variety of holidays. If you have a suggestion for a class you would like to try, please leave us feedback through an email. Classes are offered on an appointment based schedule. To check our schedule and sign up for a course please click the schedule page which can be located at the top of this page.
