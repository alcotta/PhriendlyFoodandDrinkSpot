Bartending Classes

We offer a variety of a wide variety of bartending courses, covering all the basics. We will discuss products, their origins as well as traditional and creative uses. You will walk away from our bartending courses feeling empowered at your next dinner party to show off your new mixology skills.



We offer individual courses as well as team building opportunities. Team Building opportunites are designed to bring your team together in the safest way currently possible in our changing world. Team opportunties are available for groups as small as 5 and as large as 100.
